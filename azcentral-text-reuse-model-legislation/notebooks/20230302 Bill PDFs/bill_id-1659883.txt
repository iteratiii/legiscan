
























































SENATE BILL NO.1003 (2023) - Gov contracts, restrooms


LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF IDAHO
Sixty-seventh Legislature First Regular Session - 2023

IN THE SENATE

SENATE BILL NO. 1003

BY STATE AFFAIRS COMMITTEE

AN ACT1
RELATING TO PURCHASING BY POLITICAL SUBDIVISIONS; AMENDING SECTION2

67-2802A, IDAHO CODE, TO PROVIDE THAT A PUBLIC WORKS BIDDER, OFFEROR,3
CONTRACTOR, OR SUBCONTRACTOR SHALL NOT BE REQUIRED TO PROVIDE ACCESS TO4
A MULTIPLE-OCCUPANCY RESTROOM, SHOWER FACILITY, OR CHANGING ROOM ON ANY5
BASIS OTHER THAN BIOLOGICAL SEX; AND DECLARING AN EMERGENCY AND PROVID-6
ING AN EFFECTIVE DATE.7

Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Idaho:8

SECTION 1. That Section 67-2802A, Idaho Code, be, and the same is hereby9
amended to read as follows:10

67-2802A. DISCRIMINATION IN PROCUREMENT PROHIBITED. (1) Political11
subdivisions of the state of Idaho in their procurements governed by this12
chapter shall not discriminate against, or grant preferential treatment13
to, any individual or group on the basis of race, sex, color, ethnicity,14
or national origin unless permitted by an exception described in section15
67-5909A, Idaho Code.16

(2) Unless specifically required pursuant to applicable federal law or17
regulation, a bidder, offeror, contractor, or subcontractor shall not be re-18
quired to provide access to a multiple-occupancy restroom, a multiple-oc-19
cupancy shower facility, or a multiple-occupancy changing room on any ba-20
sis other than biological sex. For purposes of this subsection, "biologi-21
cal sex" means the physical condition of being male or female as stated on a22
person's birth certificate. A bidder, offeror, contractor, or subcontrac-23
tor shall have standing to challenge a violation of the provisions of this24
subsection, and such party shall be awarded costs and attorney's fees in the25
event that such challenge prevails.26

SECTION 2. An emergency existing therefor, which emergency is hereby27
declared to exist, this act shall be in full force and effect on and after28
July 1, 2023.29
