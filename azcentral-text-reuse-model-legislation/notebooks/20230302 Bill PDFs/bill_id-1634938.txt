AN ACT 

To amend chapter 167, RSMo, by adding thereto one new section relating to middle school, high 

school, and college athletics. 
 

Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Missouri, as follows: 

     Section A.  Chapter 167, RSMo, is amended by adding thereto 1 

one new section, to be known as section 167.780, to read as 2 

follows:3 

     167.780.  1.  This act shall be known and may be cited  1 

as the "Save Women's Sports Act". 2 

     2.  No athletic team or sport designated for biological  3 

females shall be open to students that are biological males. 4 

     3.  No governmental entity, licensing or accrediting  5 

organization, or athletic association or organization shall  6 

entertain a complaint, open an investigation, or take any  7 

other adverse action against a school or institution for  8 

maintaining separate interscholastic or intramural athletic  9 

teams or sports for students that are biological females. 10 

     4.  No public school or a private middle school or high  11 

school, or a public or private institution of postsecondary  12 

education, that has biological males playing biological  13 

females' sports shall be eligible for any moneys  14 

appropriated by the general assembly. 15 

     5.  For the purposes of this section, the following  16 

terms shall mean: 17
