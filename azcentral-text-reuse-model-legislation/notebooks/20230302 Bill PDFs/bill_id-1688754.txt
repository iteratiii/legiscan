AN ACT to amend Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 49, 

Chapter 1 and Title 49, Chapter 50, relative to 
private schools. 

 
BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE: 

 SECTION 1.  Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 49, Chapter 50, Part 8, is amended by 

adding the following as a new section: 

 A private school may create a policy to regulate a student's participation in the 

school's athletic activities or events based upon a student's biological sex. 

 SECTION 2.  This act takes effect upon becoming a law, the public welfare requiring it.
