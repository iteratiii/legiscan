A BILL FOR AN ACT ENTITLED: “AN ACT REVISING LAWS REGARDING DISCRIMINATORY PRACTICES 7 

IN EDUCATION; PROVIDING THAT IT IS NOT A DISCRIMINATORY PRACTICE FOR A STUDENT TO CALL 8 

A STUDENT BY THE STUDENT'S LEGAL NAME OR REFERENCE THE STUDENT BY THE STUDENT'S 9 

SEX; AMENDING SECTION 49-2-307, MCA; AND PROVIDING AN IMMEDIATE EFFECTIVE DATE.” 10 

 11 

BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MONTANA: 12 

 13 

NEW SECTION. Section 1. Certain district policies prohibited. The trustees of a school district 14 

may not adopt a policy that subjects a student to disciplinary action for behavior that is not considered an 15 

unlawful discriminatory practice pursuant to 49-2-307(2). 16 

 17 

Section 2. Section 49-2-307, MCA, is amended to read: 18 

"49-2-307. Discrimination in education. (1) It is an unlawful discriminatory practice for an 19 

educational institution: 20 

(1)(a) to exclude, expel, limit, or otherwise discriminate against an individual seeking admission as a 21 

student or an individual enrolled as a student in the terms, conditions, or privileges of the institution because of 22 

race, creed, religion, sex, marital status, color, age, physical disability, or national origin or because of mental 23 

disability, unless based on reasonable grounds; 24 

(2)(b) to make or use a written or oral inquiry or form of application for admission that elicits or 25 

attempts to elicit information or to make or keep a record concerning the race, color, sex, marital status, age, 26 

creed, religion, physical or mental disability, or national origin of an applicant for admission, except as permitted 27 

by regulations of the commission; 28 

(Primary Sponsor)

(3)(c) to print, publish, or cause to be printed or published a catalog or other notice or advertisement 1
