STATE OF OKLAHOMA 

 

1st Session of the 59th Legislature (2023) 

 

SENATE BILL 251 By: Dahm 

 

 

 

 

 

AS INTRODUCED 

 

An Act relating to definitions and general 

provisions; limiting permissible applications of 

specified term or definition; prohibiting public 

entity from using other applications; providing for 

codification; and declaring an emergency. 

 

 

 

 

 

BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA: 

SECTION 1.     NEW LAW     A new section of law to be codified 

in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 24.1 of Title 25, unless there 

is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows: 

The only permissible applications of the terms or definitions of 

“gender” or “sex” shall be male (XY), female (XX), and intersex for 

those born with a mutation causing such abnormality.  No public 

entity in this state including but not limited to a state agency, 

political subdivision, or board of education may use any other 

application.
