
























































SENATE BILL NO.1016 (2023) - Contractors, facilities, bio sex


LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF IDAHO
Sixty-seventh Legislature First Regular Session - 2023

IN THE SENATE

SENATE BILL NO. 1016

BY STATE AFFAIRS COMMITTEE

AN ACT1
RELATING TO PURCHASING BY POLITICAL SUBDIVISIONS; AMENDING SECTION2

67-2802A, IDAHO CODE, TO PROVIDE THAT A PUBLIC WORKS CONTRACTOR SHALL3
NOT BE REQUIRED TO PROVIDE ACCESS TO A MULTIPLE-OCCUPANCY RESTROOM,4
SHOWER FACILITY, OR CHANGING ROOM ON ANY BASIS OTHER THAN BIOLOGICAL5
SEX; AND DECLARING AN EMERGENCY AND PROVIDING AN EFFECTIVE DATE.6

Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Idaho:7

SECTION 1. That Section 67-2802A, Idaho Code, be, and the same is hereby8
amended to read as follows:9

67-2802A. DISCRIMINATION IN PROCUREMENT PROHIBITED. (1) Political10
subdivisions of the state of Idaho in their procurements governed by this11
chapter shall not discriminate against, or grant preferential treatment12
to, any individual or group on the basis of race, sex, color, ethnicity,13
or national origin unless permitted by an exception described in section14
67-5909A, Idaho Code.15

(2) Unless specifically required pursuant to applicable federal law or16
regulation, no public entity or political subdivision of the state of Idaho17
shall require a contractor engaged in public works construction governed by18
this chapter to provide a multiple-occupancy restroom, shower facility, or19
changing room that is owned by or under the control of the contractor on any20
basis other than biological sex. For purposes of this subsection, "biolog-21
ical sex" means the physical condition of being male or female as stated on a22
person's birth certificate.23

SECTION 2. An emergency existing therefor, which emergency is hereby24
declared to exist, this act shall be in full force and effect on and after25
July 1, 2023.26
