 STATE OF OKLAHOMA 

 

1st Session of the 59th Legislature (2023) 

 

HOUSE BILL 2736 By: Duel 

 

 

 

 

 

AS INTRODUCED 

 

An Act relating to crimes and punishments; amending 

21 O.S. 2021, Sections 1040.75 and 1040.76, which 

relate to the display of materials harmful to minors; 

adding definition; making certain acts unlawful; and 

providing an effective date. 

 

 

 

 

 

BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:

amended to read as follows:

this title: 

1.  "Minor" means any unmarried person under the age of eighteen 

(18) years; 

2.  "Harmful to minors" means: 

a. that quality of any description, exhibition, 

presentation or representation, in whatever form, of

amended to read as follows: 

Section 1040.76.  A.  No person, including but not limited to 

any persons having custody, control or supervision of any commercial 

establishment, shall knowingly: 

1.  Display material which is harmful to minors in such a way 

that minors, as a part of the invited general public, will be

2.  Sell, furnish, present, distribute, allow to view, or

B.  No person including, but not limited to, any person having 

custody, control or supervision of a commercial establishment or 

having custody, control or supervisions of any public property, 

shall knowingly present to a minor or participate in presenting to a 

minor, with or without consideration, any drag performance. 

SECTION 3.  This act shall become effective November 1, 2023.
