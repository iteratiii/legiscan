AN ACT 

To repeal section 170.015, RSMo, and to enact in lieu thereof one new section relating to gender 

identity in schools.

     Section A.  Section 170.015, RSMo, is repealed and one new 1

     2.  Policies concerning referrals and parental  85

     3.  A school district or charter school which provides  89

     7.  No school district or charter school, or its  112

     8.  No school district or charter school, or its  119 

personnel or agents, shall provide course materials or  120

     9.  As used in this section, the following terms mean: 123 

     (1)  "Abortion", the same meaning as such term is  124

     (2)  "Abortion services": 126 

     (a)  Performing, inducing, or assisting in the  127

     (b)  Encouraging a patient to have an abortion or  130

     (3)  "Gender identity", the psychological, behavioral,  136
