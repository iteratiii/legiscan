AN ACT 

To amend chapter 167, RSMo, by adding thereto two new sections relating to athletic opportunities 

for students. 
 

Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Missouri, as follows: 

     Section A.  Chapter 167, RSMo, is amended by adding thereto 1 

two new sections, to be known as sections 167.169 and 167.172, 2 

to read as follows:3 

     167.169.  1.  This section shall be known and may be  1

     2.  An interscholastic or intramural athletic team  3

postsecondary education, shall be expressly designated as  6

assigned at birth: 8 

     (1)  "Males", "men", or "boys"; 9 

     (2)  "Females", "women", or "girls"; or 10 

     (3)  "Coeducational", "coed", or "mixed". 11 

     3.  No athletic team or sport designated for females,  12 

women, or girls shall be open to students of the male sex as  13

     167.172.  1.  For the purposes of this section,  1 

"biological sex" means the physical condition of being male  2 

or female, which is determined by a person's chromosomes,  3

     2.  In every public or private school setting in which  6
