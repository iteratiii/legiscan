AN ACT 

To amend chapter 167, RSMo, by adding thereto one new section relating to elementary and 

secondary school aged students participating in athletics. 
 

Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Missouri, as follows: 

     Section A.  Chapter 167, RSMo, is amended by adding thereto 1 

one new section, to be known as section 167.780, to read as 2 

follows:3 

     167.780.  1.  This act shall be known and may be cited  1 

as the "Save Women's Sports Act".  The provisions of this  2 

section shall apply to sports or athletic competitions,  3 

tournaments, and games intended for individuals under the  4 

age of nineteen. 5 

     2.  As used in this section, "intramural" shall mean  6 

teams, competitions, tournaments, or games that a sponsor,  7 

organizing entity, or facilitator designates is open to all  8 

genders. 9 

     3.  Every sponsor, organizing entity for sports for  10 

students in kindergarten through grade twelve, or  11 

facilitator of children or youth sports competitions shall  12 

designate each team, competition, tournament, and game as  13 

designated for exclusively males, exclusively females, or  14 

intramural. 15 

     4.  No team, competition, tournament, or game  16 

designated exclusively for females, women, or girls shall be  17 

open to participation by males, men, or boys. 18
