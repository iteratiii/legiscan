AN ACT REQUIRING PUBLIC SCHOOL EDUCATORS TO RECOGNIZE 
EACH STUDENT BY THE BIOLOGICAL GENDER OF SUCH STUDENT. 

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General 
Assembly convened: 
 

That the general statutes be amended to require public school 1 

educators to recognize each student by such student's biological gender. 2 

Statement of Purpose:   
To require public school educators to recognize each student by the 
biological gender of such student.
