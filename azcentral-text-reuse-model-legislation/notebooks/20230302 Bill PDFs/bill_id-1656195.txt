Section 1.  (1) For purposes of this section drag show means a1

performance in which:2

(a) The main aspect of the performance is a performer which exhibits3

a gender identity that is different than the performer's gender assigned4

at birth using clothing, makeup, or other physical markers; and5

(b) The performer sings, lip syncs, dances, or otherwise performs6

before an audience for entertainment.7

(2)(a) No individual under nineteen years of age shall be present at8

a drag show. Any person nineteen years of age or older who knowingly9

brings an individual under nineteen years of age to a drag show shall be10

guilty of a Class I misdemeanor.11

(b) No individual under twenty-one years of age shall be present at12

a drag show if alcoholic liquor is being served at such location.13

(3)(a) Any business, establishment, or nonprofit that hosts a drag14

show and allows an individual (i) under nineteen years of age in15

violation of subdivision (2)(a) of this section or (ii) under twenty-one16

years of age in violation of subdivision (2)(b) of this section to be17

present at a drag show shall be fined ten thousand dollars for each18

violation of this section. Any fines collected under this section shall19

be remitted to the State Treasurer for distribution in accordance with20

Article VII, section 5, of the Constitution of Nebraska.21

(b) An officer or owner of a business, establishment, or nonprofit22

that hosts a drag show and knowingly allows an individual (i) under23

nineteen years of age in violation of subdivision (2)(a) of this section24

or (ii) under twenty-one years of age in violation of subdivision (2)(b)25

of this section to be present at a drag show shall be guilty of a Class I26

misdemeanor.27

(4) No state agency that receives state funds shall use such funds28

to host a drag show.29
